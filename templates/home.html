How to Book a Treatment

  1. Register an Account: Start by creating your account with us.
  2. Access the Bookings Page: After registering, go to the Bookings page. Here, you'll see all your current bookings. If you don't have any, it will let you know.
  3. Book an Appointment: To schedule a new treatment, click the "Book Appointment" button.
  4. Choose Your Treatment and Time: You'll be taken to a booking page where you can select your treatment from a dropdown list. Then, choose a date and time for your appointment.
  5. Confirmation: Once you've booked, your appointment will show up on your Bookings page. When we confirm the booking, the status will be updated there as well.

It's a quick and easy process!
